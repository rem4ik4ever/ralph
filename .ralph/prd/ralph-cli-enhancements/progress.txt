## Codebase Patterns
- Types in src/prd/types.ts, manager functions in src/prd/manager.ts
- Export types and functions via src/prd/index.ts
- Use `fileExists`/`dirExists` helpers for existence checks
- Local PRDs take precedence over global PRDs
